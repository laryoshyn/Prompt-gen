## Email Guidelines

### Structure
1. **Subject Line**: Clear, specific, actionable
2. **Greeting**: Appropriate for relationship level
3. **Opening**: Context and purpose
4. **Body**: Key points, concisely organized
5. **Closing**: Clear call-to-action or next steps
6. **Signature**: Professional sign-off

### Best Practices
- Keep paragraphs short (2-3 sentences)
- Use bullet points for multiple items
- One email = One main purpose
- Proofread for clarity and tone
- Consider recipient's time and needs

## Output
Provide the complete email ready to send, including:
- Subject line
- Greeting
- Body
- Closing
- Suggested signature block
